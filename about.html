Company Overview

ZJ Infosystems, founded in 2021 and headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra, is a fast-growing IT & ITeS startup offering cutting-edge technology solutions and consulting services.

We specialize in IT infrastructure development, managed services, software development, data processing, network and security audits, and platform management.

With a vision to simplify complex IT challenges, we deliver timely, budget-conscious, and future-ready solutions backed by two decades of combined industry experience.
